The course

You may also download an app that summarizes what Barbara teaches in her live course. Created as a quick reference for people who have already had the course, it also serves as an easy way for you quickly to find the concepts that are explained in the book.

It requires cellular data connect or wifi, and can be found on Apple iTunes Stores for $14.99. An Android version is also available from Googleplay.

Please note that the summary provides only an insight into the thinking behind the Minto Pyramid Principle; it does not actually teach how to apply it to your work. That, alas, takes deeper explanation and practice!
